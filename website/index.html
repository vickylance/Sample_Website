Welcome to the VickylanceTutorials.site40.net

Here you will find some of the best in class Tutorials of some of the most popular Topics you will ever come across.

Ok..So we all know that there are a tons of Tutorials available on all softwares and all programming languages on the internet and also there are tons of other sites dedicated on a few or a particular software/language, which gives vast amount of knowledge but the process of Learning a software/Language is not a Joke..!! it takes a lot of pain and time to learn these things and also a lot of determination to continue learning but mastering may take even years. Well while that is true there is a reason why I have created this site. Just like you I also have took a lot of time and pain to learn which made me to find a better way to make it easy to learn. The first thing which I personally found was, just to research on the net to find the best source for learning a software or a language takes hours. Even when you find the right one you will eventually find that it is not covering some of the areas for which you have to go find out another tutorial series and waste some on it. Even if some tutorials cover all they may not give you a grasp of knowledge on the subject or it may simply be not so catchy.

Well so focusing on all these points I wanted to create a source of learning which will give a Through, Strong and mainly a much Accelerated learning process. So, how do I plan to achieve it. I will be teaching each and every topics with the following approach.

  • Mindset:-

    • First of all each and every Software/Programming Language has to be learned with a particular Mindset. Psychologically you must have a mental setup or determination on how to learn the particular Software/Programming Language. You cannot simply start to learn just by not knowing anything about it or blindly following a Tutorial. Once you prepare yourself Mentally you must know what are the things you need before starting the Tutorial.

  • A good Intro:-

    • According to me a Through Introduction is more than enough to learn at a much faster rate. Thus my introduction will be quite big but it will give you most of the information you will ever need.

  • Tutorials:-

    • Now once we are all set we are ready to Dive into the tutorial series. Well I told that there are tutorials which will cover some topics with in-depth coverage and also there are Tutorial series which will cover all the topics but may not give a through knowledge well even this tutorial may be the same way but I will be giving various links to other sites or to a particular topic covered by some other sites then and there so that even if I did not cover a topic fully or did not give a better understanding, those links will give you a good foundation.

  • Exercises:-

    • No software/language can be learnt without getting a hands on experience. And thus I will also be giving a lots and lots of exercises for you guys guys to work on from Basics to Pro, by increasing the level of difficulty of the exercise questions.

  • Where to go next?

    • This section will lead you to other Advanced level to Super-Dooper Advanced level and other Reference sites, which will help you to continue the learning process to get your expertise level to the next level. But don't worry I myself will cover most of the advanced stuffs but these links are just an add-ons for your existing knowledge.
